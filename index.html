About me

I am Gianluca, a guy who loves programming since as long as can remember. At the age of 9, discovered that I wanted to be a programmer after watching an episode of Regular Show. From that moment on, dedicated myself to learning everything I could to achieve my dream.

After 2 years of trying to understand loops and conditionals (was a bit slow) while playing with Game Maker, decided to make the step to Unity and C#. only recently managed to make a game worth of publishing (not a game designer), but the experience and hours of fun are priceless to me. As I started my fourth year of high school, became interested in web development, and that's where I am now.

Besides programming, my second passion (actually, more like position 1.01) is watching anime and reading manga. On the other hand, hate sports. Yes, I am a walking stereotype.
